Featured Organic Products

Avocado

Gains: Rich in fiber, potassium, healthy fats, and antioxidants. Supports heart and bone health, aids in cholesterol management[web:7][web:16].
Losses: May increase body heat if consumed in excess[web:16]. Avoid high intake in hot climates.

Tomatoes

Gains: High in lycopene, Vitamin C; supports immunity; promotes heart health.
Losses: Can be acidic, might not suit sensitive stomachs.

Carrots

Gains: High Vitamin A, supports vision, immune system, skin health.
Losses: Possible orange tint to skin with very high intake (rare).

Cucumbers

Gains: Hydrating, low calorie, source of Vitamin K.
Losses: Mildly cold for some; not suitable for people with kidney issues in excess.

Bananas

Gains: Good energy food, rich in potassium and Vitamin B6.
Losses: High sugar, moderation for diabetics.
